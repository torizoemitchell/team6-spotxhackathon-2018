How it Works

Claiming a Task: When you click "Claim a Task, you'll see pins on a map of 2 different types of tasks waiting to be completed in your area: community service tasks and verification tasks.

  • Community Service Tasks: Select a community service task to complete, Once you complete the task, move through the verification steps to get a fellow community member to verify the task is complete. Then enjoy your rewards!

  • Verification Tasks: Select a task completed by a fellow community member. Verify whether the task is completed or not: Be truthful! You will be rewarded whether or not the task is complete.
